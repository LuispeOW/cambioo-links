Cambioo Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Cambioo is a multiplayer game meant to be fun and fair for everyone. These guidelines explain what's expected of players and what happens if the rules are broken. They work alongside our Terms of Use, which remains the governing legal agreement.

1. Play Fair

  • No cheating, exploiting bugs or glitches, or using automation/bots/macros to play on your behalf.
  • No manipulating game state, timers, or the burning mechanic through unauthorized third-party tools.
  • No creating multiple accounts to farm rewards, dodge bans, or manipulate matchmaking/wagering outcomes.

2. Treat Other Players Respectfully

  • No harassment, hate speech, threats, or targeted abuse toward other players, whether in chat, emotes, player names, or any other in-app feature.
  • No impersonating other players, Tau Interactive LLC, or Cambioo staff.
  • No sharing another player's personal information without consent ("doxxing").

3. Keep Player Identity Appropriate

  • Player names, IDs, and any custom text must not contain slurs, sexually explicit content, or references to illegal activity.
  • We may reset or remove inappropriate player names.

4. Respect the Wagering System

  • Cambles wagered in-game are virtual and for entertainment only. Attempting to trade Cambles, accounts, or in-game outcomes for real money or goods outside the app is prohibited.
  • Colluding with other players to manipulate wager outcomes is prohibited.

5. Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness a violation of these guidelines, email tauinteractive@gmail.com with the offending player's Player ID and a description of what happened. Screenshots help.

6. Enforcement

Violations may result in, at our discretion:

  • A warning
  • Temporary suspension of online features
  • Permanent account termination, with no refund of purchased or earned Cambles

We aim to apply these guidelines consistently, but we retain final discretion over enforcement decisions given the nature of a small, actively-developed game.

7. Changes

We may update these guidelines as the game evolves. Continued use of Cambioo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.
